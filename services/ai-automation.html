The cost of manual workflows

Startups and growing medical operations lose hundreds of hours monthly to administrative tasks: transferring data from form submissions to CRM leads, sending confirmation alerts, drafting client summaries, sorting prescription logs, and coordinating project updates.

When developers or operators are tied up in manual administrative tasks, product delivery slows down, and overhead operational costs increase.

Dynamic AI Pipeline Integrations

I analyze operational loops and implement tailored automations that sync databases, trigger updates, and handle routine processes automatically.

Core Automation Implementations:

  • n8n & Make Orchestration: Linking forms, calendars, CRMs, and email engines into structured data pipelines.
  • LLM API Integration: Connecting OpenAI, Anthropic, or local LLMs to categorize leads, analyze medical notes, or generate project summaries automatically.
  • WhatsApp & SMS Bots: Deploying smart auto-responders that handle basic booking queries and queue updates without administrative delay.
