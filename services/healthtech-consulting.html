The HealthTech Digitization Goal

A custom implementation and software coordination service designed specifically for Indian doctors, clinics, and hospital departments. Instead of complex, monthly-billing SaaS frameworks, I deploy self-hosted, lightweight platforms mapping directly to practical clinic timelines.

Clinic Flow Digitization Topology

The problem with mainstream medical SaaS models

Mainstream EMR and clinic software platforms are built on complex monthly subscription licensing models. In addition to high long-term costs, these systems are designed around western medical invoicing formats, making them slow and complex for local Indian OPD consultations, where doctors typically see up to 60 patients a day.

When a clinic receptionist has to enter dozens of form inputs per patient, wait times increase, queue flow stalls, and patient satisfaction drops.

Aaryan's Clinic Digitization Ecosystem

I build and configure medical utility applications that deploy to the clinic's local server (or private cloud) as a single, one-time investment. All patient data remains private, secure, and compliant.

Core HealthTech Implementations:

  • TokenQ (Smart Queue): Visual dashboard displayed on clinic waiting screens, indicating active patients and reducing wait time anxiety.
  • RxPad Pro (Fast Prescriptions): Digital prescribing interface allowing doctors to select symptoms and remedies in under 30 seconds.
  • LabTrack Pro (Samples Flow): Direct diagnostics samples dashboard tracking blood, urine, or test runs from receipt to lab output.
  • RecallMD (Patient Retention): Automatic WhatsApp follow-up automation mapping treatment timelines to keep patients informed.
  • CareReview (Reputation Booster): Smart SMS triggers requesting reviews from satisfied patients, strengthening the clinic's Google search rankings.

Service Scope & Cost

HealthTech consulting and custom software packages start from ₹30,000 to ₹80,000 per clinic. This includes local installation, custom database provisioning, receptionist training logs, and three months of direct post-launch support.
