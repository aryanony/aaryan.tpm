Self-hosted means your data never leaves your premises. The database and application code run either on a local computer inside your clinic/office (offline-first architecture) or on your own private cloud server (VPS). No third-party company can access, throttle, or monetize your patients' medical records, customer contacts, or financial files. You maintain absolute operational sovereignty — with zero dependency on external platform uptimes or pricing changes.
Absolutely not. Unlike Practo, Zoho, Salesforce, and other subscription-based platforms that charge you every single month (plus per-booking commissions), our products are delivered under a one-time setup and lifetime licensing model. Once deployed, the software is permanently yours. The only potential third-party costs are optional server hosting (approximately ₹300–₹600/month only if you choose cloud sync) or Meta WhatsApp Business API message credits (charged directly by Meta at their actual usage rates — not by us).
We route all automated notifications (queues, follow-ups, reviews) through approved Meta WhatsApp Business API gateways (e.g. Twilio, WATI, AiSensy). Before going live, Aaryan configures your official Business Manager, registers your clinic telephone number, and submits message templates for Meta approval. This prevents your clinic number from being marked as spam or banned, adhering 100% to compliance standards.
Nothing breaks. Our core clinical tools (TokenQ and RxPad Pro) are engineered with a resilient offline-first local fallback engine. Reception check-ins and doctor consulting logs continue operating locally inside the browser's database (IndexedDB/SQLite). The moment connectivity is restored, all local data automatically synchronizes back to your clinic's backup node or private cloud server — with zero manual intervention from your receptionist.
